How to Play

Start the Game

Click the “Start” button on the homepage to begin your puzzle journey. You’ll be shown two nearly identical images.

Find the Differences

Carefully examine both images and click on the areas where you notice differences. A sound will confirm correct or incorrect guesses.

Hints

You have 3 hints per level. If you're stuck, click the “Hint” button to briefly highlight one of the remaining differences.

Progress Tracker

The counter at the top of the screen shows how many differences you’ve found out of the total.

Audio Settings

Toggle sound and background music using the buttons at the top right. Play with or without audio — your choice.

Level Completion

Once you’ve found all the differences, a message will appear. Click “OK” and then “Next” to move to the next level.

No Timer, No Stress

This is a relaxed game. There’s no countdown — just enjoy spotting the differences at your own pace.
